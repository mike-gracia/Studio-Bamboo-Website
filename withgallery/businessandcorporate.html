Business / Corporate Classes

Let Studio Bamboo help to make your business more effective by assisting your employees/teams with on-site and studio based classes. A workplace yoga program is a fabulous opportunity to bring the ancient practices of yoga into the office. Studio Bamboo can help your business by using yoga to work with issues related to physical and mental health, stress management, focus, productivity, and efficient decision making just to name a few. We can offer yoga classes in the workplace (if space available), yoga in chairs, one time seminars, special day long seminars for total well being, weekend retreats, and many more possibilities. Please e-mail or call to inquire. Private Classes The study of yoga began as a dialog between teacher and student. Studio Bamboo honors that tradition by offering Private Yoga Instruction for individual students or small groups. This is a great way to meet your individual needs for that body-mind connection, relaxation and stress management. We recommend private instruction for those who are new to yoga, those with injuries or specific limitations, or for those who wish to have a specialized consultation regarding your yoga practice. These sessions may take place in the Center or off-site. Privates can be scheduled as one-on-one, duets, or semi-privates. Studio Bamboo wants you to have the best experience possible so choosing the right teacher is important, let us help. With time and location, rates vary, please call or e-mail to inquire.
