Events

Studio Bamboo Institute of Yoga and Teacher Training School will often facilitate seminars, workshops, and teacher trainings by a variety of instructors here locally as well as internationally known Yogis. These special events are to help each practitioner delve deeper into their own practice or just learn about something new. We will offer Continued Education Credits for a small fee upon request for most events. We are excited to be able to offer such great opportunities in the coming year, hope you can join us!
