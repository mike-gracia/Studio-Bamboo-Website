About our classes

Classes at Studio Bamboo range from gentle to challenging, from relaxing restorative classes to the most intense practice imaginable. We also offer specialized classes in kids, kids with special needs, teens, pre-natal, post-natal, yoga for therapy (rehabilitative) & adaptive yoga (for those with special needs), yoga for athletes, seniors, hot classes, even laughter yoga, you will find them all and even more at Studio Bamboo. If you are new to yoga or a seasoned practitioner you will find a class to meet your needs at our Center.

In addition to our regularly scheduled classes Studio Bamboo also facilitates workshops and seminars throughout the year by our own instructional staff as well as internationally known yogis, check our events page for more information.
