New to Yoga?

What do I need to know?

We ask that you arrive at least 15 minutes prior to your first practice. This will give you time to finish registration forms, have a staff member give you a tour of the Center, get settled, and have any questions answered. When you arrive please remove your shoes and place them in the shoe cubbies in the reception area. Socks are fine until practice begins, practice is done in bare feet.
