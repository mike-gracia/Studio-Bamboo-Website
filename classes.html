Classes

Classes range from gentle to the challenging, from relaxing restorative classes to the most intense practice imaginable. Studio Bamboo will also offer specialized classes in kids, teens, pre-natal, yoga for therapy (rehabilitative), yoga for athletes, Seniors, Hot classes, even Laughter Yoga, you will find them all and even more at Studio Bamboo. If you are new to yoga or a seasoned practitioner you will find a class to meet your needs at our Center.

In addition to our regularly scheduled classes Studio Bamboo will also facilitate workshops and seminars throughout the year by our own instructional staff as well as internationally known yogis, check our events page for more information.
